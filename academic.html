In Bidii National Polytechnic, Education is the main activity that runs in the institution throughout the year. Bidii National Polytechnic was mainly started with a purpose of making the community gain educational skills and knowledge.

We offer multiple courses which are affordable and marketable out there in the community and in different countries. We go by different names,

  • SCHOOL OF BUSINESS AND EDUCATION.
  • SCHOOL OF PURE AND APPLIED SCIENCES.
  • SCHOOL OF HEALTH SCIENCES.
  • SCHOOL OF ENGINEERING AND TECHNOLOGY.
  • DEPARTMENT OF TEXTILE TECHNOLOGY
because we touch every field of work in the world.

Do not worry of the grade you got after High School, because everyone is considered in regardless of the grade they achieved. This is because, under every department listed above, therbe are multiple levels of study one can choose i.e

  • Postgraduate programs
  • Undergraduate programs
  • Diploma programs
  • Certificate programs
  • Artisan programs
  • Short courses
This is to ensure that nobody is left out from getting education in Bidii National Polytechnic.

In Bidii National Polytechnic, there is usually 99% of the students who graduate per year. We encourage our trainees by creating a barazas where they can give their opinions and also there are several stations of guidance and counselling in our institution.

In Bidii National Polytechnic there is zero tolerance to indiscipline behaviours and also immoral behaviours may lead to suspension or even expulsion of both trainers and trainees.
