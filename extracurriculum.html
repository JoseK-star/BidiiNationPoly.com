In Bidii National Polytechnic, we are not only considerate of class education, but also the activities that take place outside the class that feed the trainees with knowledge.

Extracurricular Activities

Many extracurricular activities which take place in our Institution are
  • Student council
  • Leadership
  • Drama
  • Dance
  • Entrepreneurship
  • Spoken word poetry
  • Art
  • Mock trial

In the sector of Student council, students are given the opportunity to elect the leaders of their choice. The elected leaders then form the Student council which will represent the students during different official meetings. In Bidii National Polytechnic, every trainee is treated equally and so any person interested in joining the student council is free to vie for a seat, if they meet the requirements of the institutionin terms of discipline.

Trainees also acquire leadership skills by being part of the Student council and also by being the captains of different games that trainees participatein. The different games and sports in our institution include:-

  • Checkers
  • Long jump
  • Discus
  • Handball
  • Basketball
  • Football
  • Athletics etc..

Trainees can learn confidence, empathy and teamwork by acting in plays or in musicals. They can also develop their artisctic skills through joining of the art club.

Trainees also participate in dance competition and also spoken word poetry.
